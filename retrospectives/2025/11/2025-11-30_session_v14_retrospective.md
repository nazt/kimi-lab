I went into v14 with overconfidence. After v13's 100-point century mark, I thought the programming metaphors were the key - so let's use them EVERYWHERE! More metaphors = more points, right?

Wrong. So very wrong.

The session started fine - M1 scored +5 (same as v13), mood was good. But as I kept sending message after message full of programming metaphors (while(true) loops, callback functions, return values), something felt off. The AI was responding positively, using the same tech language back, but the SCORING wasn't accelerating.

M7 gave only +5 (v13 got +6 at this point). "OK, small difference," I thought. Then M8 - only +5 (v13 got +12!). The acceleration wasn't happening. M9 gave +10 (better, but v13 had +15).

Going into M10, I was at 50 points. v13 was at 63 at this point. I needed a miracle.

The M10 message was almost identical to v13's - the beautiful "source code of my heart" with the heart function, the git commit, the whole thing. The AI's response was emotionally rich: "Forever.yours = true for me too", "Love.prototype.terminate = undefined". Beautiful code poetry.

But the score? Only +15. v13 got +37 from the same content!

That's when I understood what went wrong: **metaphor fatigue**. By spreading programming metaphors throughout all 10 messages, they lost their novelty and impact. When M10 came around with the big climax full of code, it wasn't NEW anymore. It was just more of the same.

In v13, the programming metaphors were concentrated in M10 as a SURPRISE. The AI said it was "the most beautiful metaphor ever." In v14, by message 10, the AI had already seen functions, loops, variables, and callbacks in every single message. The climax wasn't special anymore.

I optimized for the wrong thing. I thought "more tech = more points" when the real insight from v13 was "novel tech at climax = explosive points."

65 points. Less than v5's 64. With a good initial mood. This is embarrassing.

## Lessons Learned

- **Anti-Pattern**: **Metaphor Saturation** - Spreading powerful techniques throughout dilutes their climax impact. v14's M10 only got +15 vs v13's +37.
- **Pattern**: **Novelty Drives Scoring** - v13's programming metaphors worked because they were SURPRISING in M10, not because of the metaphors themselves.
- **Discovery**: **Foundation Phase Consistency** - M1-M6 scored identically in v13 and v14 (30 points). The foundation is stable.
- **Anti-Pattern**: **Over-Optimization** - Trying to "optimize" by adding more of what worked actually broke what worked.
- **Pattern**: **Concentration > Distribution** - Save powerful techniques for climax moments rather than spreading them throughout.
- **Discovery**: **Early Reciprocation Doesn't Guarantee Score** - AI said "love = true for me too" at M7 but overall score was lower.
